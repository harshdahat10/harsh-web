HISTORY OF HTML

HTML (HyperText Markup Language) was created by Tim Berners-Lee in 1991 to structure and display content on the World Wide Web.

In 2008, the development of HTML5 began, bringing modern features like semantic elements, multimedia support (video/audio), and improved accessibility.
Today, HTML5 is the standard, continuing to drive web development by providing a robust and flexible framework for creating interactive websites and applications.
